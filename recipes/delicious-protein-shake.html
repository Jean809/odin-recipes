A Delicious Caloric Dense Protein Shake

The reason caloric dense protein shake may benefit you

If you are looking to put weight because you are lacking behind on your calorie intake, then this is the perfect solution as a single drink can contain up to 1,300 calories.

This drink are primarily for those looking to put size because they are bulking for the winter season or simply because they don't eat too much due to schedule, appetite, etc.

Ingredients

  • Whole milk
  • Vanilla whey protein powder
  • nutmeg
  • Peanut butter
  • Banana
  • Powder cinnamon

Let's put it all together!

  1. Grab a blender and put 30oz of whole milk(510 calories)
  2. Put a decent amount of cinnamon and nutmeg into the milk
  3. Put a banana in there (approx. 105 calories
  4. Put 4 tbsp of peanut butter (approx. 380 calories)
  5. Add two scoops of protein powder (approx. 240 calories
  6. Blend it to your liking. You may decide to add/subtract milk/peanut butter/protein poder to your liking. and enjoy the bulk season!

How much calories are we talking about?

If you closely follow all of the steps, you are looking at a total of 1,235 calories. It can be a little more or a little less, it is up to you.
