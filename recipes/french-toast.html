Simple guide to French Toast

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices of bread
  • milk
  • cinnamon
  • butter
  • nutmeg
  • sugar
  • 1 egg

How to put it all together!

  1. mix 2 cups of milk with nutmeg, the egg, and the cinnamon
  2. throw some butter into a hot pan and wait for the butter to melt
  3. After the butter is melt, wet a slice of bread into the mixed milk and throw it into the hot pan
  4. Wait for it to be ready and take it out.
  5. Try it out, if it taste good then wet the other slices of bread and do the same; otherwise put more sugar to the mixed milk
  6. Put some syrup and enjoy!
