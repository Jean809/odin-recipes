Simple Guide to Pancakes

Pancakes are great for bulking

When you are bulking, you need food that offers the most amount of calories for x portion

This is why pancakes are a great deal, because it can allow you to intake a great amount of calories for a little portion

which can lead you to store a lot of fat, protein, and carbs for your next workout session.

Ingredients to get started

  • pancakes box mix
  • 3 eggs
  • milk
  • cinnamon in powder
  • nutmeg
  • sugar
  • butter

Steps to finish your pancakes

  1. Mix your milk with the box mix, eggs, cinnamon, nutmeg, and sugar(The sugar, cinnamon and nutmeg amount is based on your taste)
  2. Place one tbsp of butter into a hot pan
  3. Put a decent amount of your mixed milk into the hot pan
  4. Wait for the pancake to get a golden color and then flip. Keep doing this until it reaches your desired taste
  5. Take out the pancake and repeat step 3 until you run out of the mixed milk
  6. Put some butter and syrup on top of your pancake and enjoy!
